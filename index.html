The Clinical Philosophy
The body is an ecosystem.
Medicine got too singular.

The body is an ecosystem of intricate systems that never function individually — but in harmony, or in some cases disharmony. Medicine got too singular. The five pillars are that intervention. In sequence. With the evidence base behind every recommendation. Built by a clinician who has lived both the practice and the patient experience.
